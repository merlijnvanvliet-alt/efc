Why Electric Flying

Three reasons that change everything

2,000+

Regional airports ready to connect

Europe alone has over 2,000 regional airports. Electric flying unlocks point-to-point travel between these hubs — no hub-and-spoke detours, no wasted time.

200–500

Kilometres. The sweet spot for electric.

Current battery technology already covers the most common short-haul distances. This is the segment where electric aviation is ready to compete today.

1,000

Kilometres within reach as technology advances

As batteries improve and renewable energy scales, a significant portion of fossil fuel flights up to 1,000 km will be replaced by clean electric alternatives.

Our Vision

Electric flying as mainstream transportation

We envision a future where electric flying is not a niche technology but a mainstream transportation option for millions of travellers across Europe.

EFC understands that making electric aviation a success requires close cooperation with the existing aviation industry. We do not reinvent — we connect and accelerate.

Founded in November 2021 at the Automotive Campus in Helmond, EFC operates from Airport Teuge and brings together manufacturers, airports, training organisations, and innovators.
