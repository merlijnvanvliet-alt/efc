Why Electric Flying

The opportunity
in three numbers

Europe's regional aviation infrastructure is ready. The technology is proven. What's needed is the network to connect them — and that is exactly what EFC builds.

2,000+

Regional airports in Europe

Electric flying unlocks point-to-point travel between Europe's regional hubs — eliminating hub-and-spoke detours and reducing travel time significantly.

200–500 km

The sweet spot for electric

Current battery technology already covers the most-travelled short-haul distances. This is the segment where electric aviation is ready to compete today.

1,000 km

Within reach as technology scales

As batteries improve and renewable energy becomes more available, a significant share of fossil fuel flights under 1,000 km can be replaced by electric alternatives.

Vision

Electric flying as a mainstream option for regional travel

We envision a future where electric aviation is not a niche technology but a standard transportation option connecting Europe's regions sustainably and efficiently.

EFC understands that success requires close cooperation with the existing aviation industry. We do not replace — we connect and accelerate. Our six working groups cover the full value chain: from aircraft manufacturing and certification to infrastructure, training, and entrepreneurship.
