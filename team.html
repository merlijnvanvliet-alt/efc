Our People

The team behind
Electric Flying Connection

EFC is driven by a dedicated board and a network of experienced advisors, united by the conviction that electric aviation is the future of regional transport.

Section 01

EFC Board

JdJ
Chairman
Jurjen de Jong

Jurjen is a results-driven entrepreneur (e.g. co-founder of NRG2Fly), advisor, and investor with over 20 years of experience in eMobility and Renewable Energy. He has a proven track record of success in these fields and is committed to driving change toward a more sustainable future.

EFC Chairman
GG
Secretary
Gerben Groothuis

As new business director of Twente Airport, Gerben has the ambition of being a nursery for sustainable aviation solutions. Electric Flight is one of the main avenues. The aviation market makes a shift towards zero-emission and advanced mobility solutions. Gerben has substantial experience in leading new business development and cross border business arrangements across a multitude of industries.

EFC Secretary
MvV
Treasurer
Merlijn van Vliet

Merlijn is a creative entrepreneur with a passion for building sustainable brands. He is the co-owner of E-Flight Academy, Europe's first sustainable flight academy. With his combination of creativity, business acumen and passion for sustainability, Merlijn is helping to shape the future of the electric flight industry.

EFC Treasurer

Board Advisors

MS
Board Advisor
Maarten Steinbuch

Maarten is a high-tech systems scientist and a well-known keynote speaker. He holds the position of Scientific Director at EindhovenEngine, and he's Distinguished Professor at TU Eindhoven. He is a leading voice in the field and regularly speaks at conferences and events to share his insights and knowledge.

TU Eindhoven
BdB
Board Advisor
Bastiaan de Buijne

Bastiaan is a purpose-driven international advisor with deep expertise in navigating geopolitical and societal transformations within complex regulatory landscapes. His background spans aerospace, energy, mobility, critical infrastructure and defense. With a passion for people, positive change and public good, Bastiaan brings strategic insight to help shape the future of sustainable aviation.

Board Advisor
NW
Board Advisor
Nick Waterdrinker

Nick is Managing Director of Point b BV, energy-transition consultants providing strategic business development services. He chairs the North Sea Heliports Alliance, connecting Aberdeen, Den Helder, Emden, Esbjerg, Humberside and Avinor Stavanger Airport. With his background as Chief Operations Officer in offshore subsea operations, Nick brings valuable expertise in maritime aviation and sustainable energy transition.

He brings his wealth of experience and his passion for these areas to his work and is dedicated to promoting sustainable transportation and connecting people to the industry.
